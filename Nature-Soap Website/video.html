Soapberries Live

We take Nature-Soap Soapberries into the community to put their cleaning power to the test in front of real people. We get asked all the time about really specific messes and if the cleaning power of Soapberries will do the job. If we don't know, we say "Let's put it to the test!"

Some Key Facts:

  • Soapberries are antimicrobial, anti-bacterial and also kill dustmites!
  • Soapberries can be used for more than just laundry, it excels at cleaning surfaces and glass!
  • As powerful as these berries are, they are gentle enough for sensitive skin and infants!
  • Soapberries are ecologically sound, and easily biodegradable!
