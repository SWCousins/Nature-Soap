Go Team Nature-Soap!

Nature-Soap

Nature-Soap has a Triple Bottom Line business model, that means that we're not just here to make a buck, we are here to make a positive difference Ecologically as well as Socially. In fact, our mission statement defines our number one priority as:
"To make a positive difference in the lives of others with the power of Soapberries"
This means going out of our way to give these amazing berries to the less fortunate, as well as anyone who is interested in trying their incredible cleaning power for themselves. Unlike other Soapberry suppliers, we leave the berry intact with the seed inside. This means that there is no need to use inconvenient cloth bags, and every box comes with dozens of potential trees just waiting to be planted. They call it bad for Business, we call it good for the Environment.

We take our responsibility to People and Planet seriously, does your detergent company?
